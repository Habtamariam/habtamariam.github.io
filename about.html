About Me

The engineer, researcher, and teacher behind the work

A closer look at my background, education, and what I care about professionally.

Summary

Engineer first, researcher and teacher by discipline

I currently design and build the executive dashboard for ICSMIS, a UNDP-funded platform for Ethiopia's Federal Civil Service Commission, owning the project end to end. Alongside that, I work as a Software Business Analyst on SmartOffice, a national enterprise platform deployed across major Ethiopian public institutions.

I hold an MSc in Computer Networks and Communications, where my thesis used machine learning to detect and mitigate DDoS attacks in SDN-Edge-IoT environments — later published in IEEE Access. I lecture in Information Technology, peer-review for IEEE Access, IEEE IoT Journal and several international conferences, and I am a Training-of-Trainers (ToT) graduate of the SNA Africa Project's digitalization programme.

What ties it together: I like systems that connect many moving parts — modules, networks, people, institutions — into something a decision-maker, student, or citizen can actually use.
